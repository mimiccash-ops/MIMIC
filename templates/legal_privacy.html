Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

MIMIC ("we", "us", "the Platform") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our copy trading platform.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

Data Type Purpose
Username Account identification
Email address Account recovery, notifications
Phone number (optional) Two-factor authentication, recovery
Password hash Account security (we never store plaintext passwords)

2.2 Trading Data

  • Trade history (symbols, entry/exit prices, PnL)
  • Balance history for performance tracking
  • Trading preferences and settings

2.3 Exchange API Keys

  • API keys are encrypted using Fernet symmetric encryption
  • We only request trading permissions (never withdrawal)
  • Keys are stored securely and never logged in plaintext

2.4 Technical Data

  • IP address (for security and geo-compliance)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and timestamps

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Trading Services: Execute copy trades on your behalf
  • Account Security: Prevent unauthorized access and fraud
  • Notifications: Send trade alerts, system updates, and important notices
  • Compliance: Meet legal obligations and geo-blocking requirements
  • Improvement: Analyze usage to improve the Platform

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Cryptocurrency Exchanges: Only API credentials necessary for trading
  • Service Providers: Hosting, email, and security services under strict confidentiality
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

5. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security:

  • Fernet encryption for API keys (AES-128-CBC with HMAC)
  • Scrypt password hashing
  • HTTPS/TLS for all connections
  • Rate limiting and brute-force protection
  • Regular security audits
  • Principle of least privilege for data access

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while account is active
  • Trade History: Retained for 2 years for reporting
  • Security Logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is anonymized or deleted within 30 days

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object: Opt-out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us through the Platform's support system.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Security (CSRF protection)
  • Preferences and settings

We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

10. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or Platform notification.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through the Platform's support system or messaging feature.
